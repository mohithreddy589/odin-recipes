Maggi

Description about the recipe

Nothing makes a Desi kid more nostalgic than a bowl of maggi. While traditionally it’s simply made with the spice packet that comes along with it and some veggies, I created this super-spicy elevated version that I know you’ll love. You’ll never want to eat maggi the regular way again!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Taste maker
  • Oil
  • Ketchup
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Heat up oil in a sauce pan on medium heat and add in your chopped yellow onions. Sauté until translucent.
  2. Add in all of your desired veggies such as corn, peas, and green cabbage, and sauté for a couple of minutes until tender.
  3. In a bowl, mix together your ketchup, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sriracha, sambal, kashmiri mirch, salt, garam masala, cumin, and garlic powder.
  4. Add your sauce mixture to your onions and cook for a couple of minutes on a low medium heat.
  5. Add in your water and ramen/maggi noodles and cook until the ramen is fully cooked through.
  6. Garnish with green onions.
